Why Oak

Finance that feels alive

🏦

Live Bank Data

Connects directly to your Danske Bank account via secure open banking. Your transactions appear automatically — no manual entry.

🌱

Your Financial Tree

A pixel-art oak grows or withers based on your real financial health score. Overspend and it goes bare. Save well and it flourishes.

🎯

Savings Goals

Set targets for anything — a down payment, new laptop, or a wedding ring. Watch progress bars fill as your savings grow.

📅

Transaction History

A clean, date-grouped view of every purchase. Netto, DSB, KAB — all in one place, always up to date.

💚

Financial Health Score

A single percentage that captures how well you're managing your money this month. Simple, honest, motivating.

🔒

Secure by Design

OAuth + MitID authentication. Read-only access. Your bank credentials never touch Oak's servers.
